About SPSC

Our hallmark is Excellence driven by Passion!

The Sindh Public Service Commission (SPSC) is a constitutional body responsible for recruiting qualified professionals for the provincial civil services. Established under Article 242 of the Constitution of Pakistan, SPSC operates with a mission to select the best talent through a transparent and merit-based process.

Our Mission

To select competent, dedicated, and professional individuals for the civil service through a fair, transparent, and merit-based recruitment process.

Our Vision

To be a model institution that sets the highest standards of governance, integrity, and efficiency in public service recruitment.

Our Values

Integrity, Transparency, Meritocracy, Equal Opportunity, Professionalism, and Continuous Improvement.
